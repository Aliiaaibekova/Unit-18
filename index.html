About Us

Canan is an Australian registered psychologist. Early in her career, she gained experience working at schools around the world with teenagers/ young adults, parents and teachers. Later on, she focused more on adult psychology. She worked both in public hospital and private health in Sydney, Australia where she realized the importance of providing culturally competent and culturally sensitive therapy.
